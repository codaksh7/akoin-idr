01 — About IDR

An industry-led institute for digital, cyber and AI risk.

The Institute of Digital Risk is an industry-led training and deployment institute for digital, cyber and technology risk roles in regulated, high-consequence environments — from financial services to critical national infrastructure.

We bridge the growing gap between academic insight and operational reality. Through an exclusive UK university partnership, IDR embeds research-grade frameworks into practical, employer-ready programmes — creating practitioners who don't just understand risk, but can manage and mitigate it at scale.
